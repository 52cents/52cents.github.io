How does the 30-day free trial work?

Each plan is available for a 30 days trial period except the free plan which is free forever. After your trial period expires you'll be asked to submit your payment details. If you cancel within 30 days of signing up we wonâ€™t charge you anything. If you cancel after the first 30 days you will be charged for the current month and never again.

Can I change plan later?

Yes, you may move up and down between plan levels as long as your usage qualifies for that plan. When you switch to another plan your old billing cycle is canceled and a new billing cycle starts for the new plan from that day.

What if I decide to cancel?

You can cancel any time from within your 52cents account.

How does the payment process work?

Once your 30 day trial expires you'll be asked to proceed with payment details. On clicking 'proceed to checkout' button you will be directed to our merchant page at 2Checkout where you'll complete your payment.
